The Breaking Bad Bank Game


There are four pictures of Breaking Bad characters below. By clicking on a character you will add a specific amount of money to your bank roll. You win by matching your bank roll to the winning bank roll, You lose the game if your bank roll goes above the random number because you will incur unnecessary laundering costs. The dollar value of each character is kept hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the dollar values on each character.

CLICK ANY CHARACTER TO START!

(all dollar values are obviously in millions...these are world class entreprenuers after all.)
